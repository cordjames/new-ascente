FUND TYPES

Conventional Funds

Conventional funds are an investment scheme that pools money from many investors who share the same financial objectives, preferred investment strategy and risk tolerance levels. The pooled money is then invested by a professional fund manager into a diversified portfolio of authorized investments approved by the Securities Commission Malaysia.

Shariah-Compliant Funds

A Shariah-compliant fund is a collective investment fund that offer investors the opportunity to invest in a diversified portfolio of Shariah-Compliant shares and fixed income securities, as well as other Shariah-compliant money market instruments. These funds are operating in compliance with Shariah principles.

Wholesale Funds

Our Wholesale Funds are created exclusively for Corporate clients who have unique investment objectives and require higher returns in their cash reserves.
